Wild Trees Pack 1.4
32 prefabs for your projects

Pack of detailed wild trees, wild bushes and wild stumps.
All the trees, bushes and stumps of the package created in the 
"TreeCreator", fully editable and respond to the "WindZone".
The package contains diffuse and normal textures.

Wild Trees Pack contains:
  • 10 leaves trees
  • 10 dead trees
  • 4 leaves bushes
  • 4 dead bushes   
  • 4 dead stumps
  • 5 terrain textures
  • 1 skybox

Textures sizes:
  • branch - 2048x2048 (diffuse/normal)
  • dead branch - 2048x2048 (diffuse/normal)
  • bark - 1024x2048 (diffuse/normal)
  • dead bark - 1024x2048 (diffuse/normal)
  • dead break - 512x512 (diffuse/normal)
  • terrain - 2048x2048 (diffuse/normal)
